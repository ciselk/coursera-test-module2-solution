OUR MENU

WONTONS
Similar to Italian tortellini. Boiled and served in soup or sometimes deep-fried. Filling of wontons can be minced pork or diced shrimp.

DUMPLINGS
Consist of minced meat and chopped vegetables wrapped in a thin dough skin.Popular fillings are mince pork, diced shrimp, ground chicken, beef, and vegetables.

MA PO TOFU
A spicy and hot taste which comes from pepper powder. The milky tofu is enriched with brownish red ground beef and chopped green onion.
